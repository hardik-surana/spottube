About Spottube

What is Spottube?

Spottube is an attempt at making an interactive web application using data fetched from different APIs. The application was created as the final project for CS 5610 Web Development course under the guidance of Professor Richard Rasala. This page uses APIs from Spotify and YouTube to search for content and display it on the page. I have used HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery and Bootstrap to create and style this web application...

Spotify is a commercial music streaming service that provides digital rights management-restricted content from record labels including Sony, EMI, Warner Music Group, and Universal. Music can be browsed or searched by artist, album, genre, playlist, or record label...

YouTube is a video-sharing website which allows users to upload, view, and share videos, and it makes use of Adobe Flash Video and HTML5 technology to display a wide variety of user-generated and corporate media video. Available content includes video clips, TV clips, music videos, and other content such as video blogging, short original videos, and educational videos...
